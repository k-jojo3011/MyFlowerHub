Our Promise

At Skyler Fresh Exporters, we are committed to bridging Kenya’s premium floriculture to the world. From the fertile highlands, we source and export fresh-cut blooms, bouquets, and foliage across global markets.

Our Mission

To export the highest-quality fresh-cut flowers while building strong, ethical relationships with growers and global buyers.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s most trusted and innovative exporter of floriculture products.
