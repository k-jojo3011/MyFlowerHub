What We Do

Sourcing & Aggregation

We partner with certified flower farms across Kenya to select the best varieties, stems, and batches for export. Our team ensures each flower meets premium quality standards, focusing on freshness, uniformity, and ethical farming practices. By aggregating blooms from multiple farms, we maintain a consistent supply that meets the demands of international markets.

Quality Assurance & Certification

All our partner farms follow strict agronomic best practices, ensuring healthy, high-quality flowers from farm to export. We conduct regular inspections and maintain rigorous quality checks at every stage—harvesting, packing, cooling, and storage. Our flowers meet international standards and comply with all necessary export documentation, giving our clients confidence in consistent, premium-quality blooms. Certifications from recognized bodies validate our commitment to sustainability, safety, and traceability across the supply chain.

Packaging & Cold Chain Logistics

We oversee the entire post-harvest process, ensuring flowers are carefully packed, rapidly cooled, and stored under optimal conditions. Every step—from export documentation to freight scheduling—is closely monitored to maintain freshness, quality, and timely delivery to international markets.

Global Export & Distribution

We provide tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of each buyer. From selecting specific flower varieties and determining optimal pack rates to scheduling deliveries according to your timelines, our team ensures every order aligns with your preferences. By customizing our services, we make it easy for clients to receive exactly what they need—fresh, high-quality flowers delivered reliably and on schedule.

Customized Solutions for Buyers

We tailor pack rates, varieties and delivery schedules to your needs.
