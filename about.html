About Skyler Fresh Exporters

Our Mission

To deliver world-class Kenyan flowers and bouquets—fresh, traceable, and sustainably sourced—with seamless global logistics, enabling our partners abroad to delight their customers with premium blooms.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and transparency in every transaction.
  • Quality: We deliver only the freshest, finest, and most consistent produce to our clients.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology and continuous improvement to redefine agricultural trade.
  • Sustainability: We promote eco-friendly and socially responsible farming practices.
  • Partnership: We build lasting relationships with growers, clients, and communities.
  • Excellence: We set high standards in service delivery and operational efficiency.

Sustainability

At Skyler Fresh, we are committed to responsible practices across all aspects of our operations:

  • Ethical labor and fair sourcing practices at farm level.
  • Minimizing environmental footprint via efficient logistics, waste reduction, and responsible packaging.
  • Investing in local community development and empowering our grower partners.
